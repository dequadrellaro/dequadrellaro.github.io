Hi! I am a Doctoral Student at the University of Helsinki under the supervision of [Fan Yang](https://sites.google.com/site/fanyanghp/) and [Juha Kontinen](https://www.mv.helsinki.fi/home/jkontine/). My research area revolves around dependence logic, inquisitive logic, universal algebra and duality theory. I am also interested in the history of ideas, especially in history of mathemtics and history of philosophy. I studied Mathematical Logic at the University of Amsterdam and Philosophy at the University of Milan.
